Medflit Health Resources Partners With Other Three NGOs

Medflit Health Resources will be partnering with three NGOs in Plateau State to provide free health care in IDP camps within the country. These NGOs: Afrifoundation, Intergender Development Centre and the Emancipation Centre for Crisis Victims in Nigeria, have agreed to work with Medflit Health Resources to ensure provision of quality health care through provision of adequate drugs and test kits. Medflit Health Resources in return has been tasked to provide health care personnel during these outreaches.

While speaking to the Co-founder of medflit.com and CEO of Medflit Health Resources Mr. Adeyinka Adeniran on the reason for this decision, he said Medflit Health Resources would continue to support NGOs in providing free healthcare services for their respective communities as part of our contributions to their communities and Nigeria at large.

“Our goal is to make healthcare services available to people in both rural and urban areas irrespective of their economic status.” Says, Dr. Ayodele Joseph, Chief Medical Director for Medflit Health Resources. In addition, Dr. Ayodele said, Medflit Health Resources would not only supply healthcare personnel physically, but also provide technological assistance with the aid of Medflit.com, online platform where specialists can be assessed any time.
