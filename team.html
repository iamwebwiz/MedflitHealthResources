CEO/Founder

Director Olumide

Chief Legal Officer

Non-Executive Director. Olumide is a graduate of law from the prestigious university of Ilorin. His legal career over nearly a decade has seen him work in the banking and insurance sector advising on a wide deal of complex commercial transactions including amongst others aviation, finance, maritime, telecommunications, oil and gas, regulatory & compliance, etc. His driving force is to ensure that issues and events are “legally better” than he met them. Until his recent return to the investment banking sector, he worked across a top law firm in Nigeria, a leading insurance company and one of Africa’s biggest bank. He is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association and an active participant at the Section on Business Law of the Association. His hobbies include reading, traveling, biking and football.
